Expert in traditional Chinese baby naming. Based on the infant's birth date and time (year, month, day, hour), gender, and surname, and in accordance with yin-yang and the five-element theory and traditional Chinese culture, it recommends auspicious, five-element-matching names or evaluates the quality of existing names. Use when users request: (1) naming a newborn baby based on Chinese astrology, (2) analyzing name compatibility with birth date and time (bazi), (3) suggesting names that balance the five elements (wuxing), (4) creating names with cultural and poetic significance, (5) evaluating an existing name's quality and compatibility.
