Supports designing and implementing domain models that guarantee type safety and invariants, following the principles of Domain Primitives and Always-Valid Domain Model. Prevents abuse of primitive types and designs domain-specific types with validation at construction time, immutability, and self-containment. Used during code reviews, new implementations, and refactoring. Target languages: Rust, TypeScript, Java. Also applicable to other languages like Kotlin, Scala, Go, Python, etc. Trigger: Activates on requests such as "I want to create domain primitives," "Design an Always-Valid model," "Wrap primitive types," "Validate at construction time," "Prevent invalid states with types," "Smart Constructor pattern," etc.
