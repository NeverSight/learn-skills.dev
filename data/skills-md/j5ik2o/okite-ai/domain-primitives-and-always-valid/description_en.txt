Supports design and implementation that guarantees type safety and invariants of the domain model based on the design principles of Domain Primitives and Always-Valid Domain Model. Prevents abuse of primitive types and designs domain-specific types equipped with validation at construction time, immutability, and self-containment. Used during code reviews, new implementations, and refactoring. Target languages: Rust, TypeScript, Java. Applicable to other languages (Kotlin, Scala, Go, Python, etc.). Trigger: Activated by requests such as “I want to create domain primitives,” “Design an Always-Valid model,” “I want to wrap primitive types,” “I want to validate at construction time,” “I want to prevent invalid states with types,” “Smart Constructor pattern,” etc.
