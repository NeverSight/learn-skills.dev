Code review and design support based on Demeter’s Law (Law of Least Knowledge). Detects chained method calls (train wrecks) and converts them to designs that talk only to direct friends. Promotes reduced coupling and improved maintainability. Use during code review, new implementation, or refactoring when object coupling is deep. Target languages: Java, Kotlin, Scala, TypeScript, Python, Ruby, Go, Rust. Triggered by coupling-related requests such as “Demeter’s Law,” “reduce chained calls,” “fix train wreck,” “lower coupling,” “too many dot chains,” “Law of Least Knowledge,” or “Law of Demeter.”
