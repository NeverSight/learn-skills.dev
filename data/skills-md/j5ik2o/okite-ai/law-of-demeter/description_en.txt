Code review and design assistance based on Demeter’s Law (Law of Least Knowledge). Detects chained method calls (Train Wreck) and converts them to designs that talk only to immediate friends. Promotes lower coupling and improved changeability. Use during code review, new implementation, or refactoring when object coupling is deep. Supported languages: Java, Kotlin, Scala, TypeScript, Python, Ruby, Go, Rust. Triggered by coupling-related requests such as “Demeter’s Law,” “reduce chained calls,” “fix Train Wreck,” “lower coupling,” “too many dot chains,” “Law of Least Knowledge,” or “Law of Demeter.”
