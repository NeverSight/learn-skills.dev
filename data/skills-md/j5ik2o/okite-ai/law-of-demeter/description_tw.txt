Code review and design assistance based on Demeter’s Law (Law of Least Knowledge). Detects chained method calls (Train Wreck) and refactors them into designs that talk only to immediate friends. Promotes them into designs that talk only to immediate friends. Promotes lower view. implementation, or refactoring when object coupling is deep. Supported languages: Java, Kotlin, Scala, TypeScript, Python, Ruby, Go, Rust.觸發器：「德米特定律」、「減少鍊式定律」、「修復列車殘骸」、「降低等值」、「降低點數」、「最小耦合資訊」、「減少相關性」、「降低等值」的資訊
