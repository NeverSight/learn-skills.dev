Professional skills for analyzing financial statements of listed companies. Used to analyze PDF-format financial reports (quarterly, semi-annual, annual), extract data from the three major statements, calculate multi-dimensional financial indicators such as profitability, solvency, operational capacity, cash flow quality, and growth, conduct DuPont and trend analyses, generate investment ratings and recommendations. Supports generating professional analysis reports in Markdown and Excel formats. Use this skill when users request financial statement analysis, calculation of financial indicators like ROE/ROA, assessment of company financial health, or generation of investment advice.
