Video Storyboard Generator supports brainstorming, user-preference memory, 15-second shot rule, character prompt generation, and SFX/BGM suggestions. It creates complete storyboard scripts with camera-movement guidance, scene design, narration, and character descriptions. Offers Minimal Mode (content description) and Pro Mode (detailed camera work). Use cases: tutorials, book introductions, reviews, plots, product demos, data reports. Triggers when the user says "make storyboard," "storyboard script," or "video storyboard."
