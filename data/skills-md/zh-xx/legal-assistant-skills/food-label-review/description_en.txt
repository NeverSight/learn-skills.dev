Pre-packaged food label compliance auditing skills, used to verify whether food labels comply with GB 7718 (General Rules for Pre-packaged Food Labels) and GB 28050 (General Rules for Pre-packaged Food Nutrition Labels). Applicable scenarios: (1) when a user submits a food label image, text, or document for compliance audit; (2) when the user mentions keywords such as "food label audit," "label compliance," "nutrition label review," "GB 7718," or "GB 28050"; (3) when the user requests to check whether the food label has missing items, errors, or violation risks. Supports the 2011 and 2025 versions of the standards.
