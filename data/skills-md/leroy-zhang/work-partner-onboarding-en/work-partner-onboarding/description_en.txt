QoderWork Work Partner onboarding. Trigger when the user first launches QoderWork, wants to set up their work system, or says things like "help me organize my work", "initialize", "get to know me", "set up my work partner". Also triggers when the user says "re-learn about me" or "update my work setup". This is an onboarding flow for non-technical office workers — it learns about the user's work through conversation, then generates a work profile and personalized workflow skill.
