Remove unnecessary complexity from the UI: cut down on excessive controls, redundant copy, unneeded confirmations, visual noise, and detailed settings that aren’t required on first load. Use this when users say “it’s cluttered,” “there’s too much information,” “too many confirmation dialogs,” “too many settings,” “I want it simpler,” or “the explanations are wordy.” Do not use it for issues that are only about inconsistent behavior or unclear targets.
