Needs-Mining Assistant: automatically searches social platforms (X/Twitter, Reddit, Hacker News, etc.) for complaints, pain points, and unmet needs, then analyzes them to surface product opportunities, competitive insights, and market intelligence.

Trigger phrases:
- “Dig up user needs in the XX field/product for me.”
- “Analyze user complaints about XX product.”
- “Show me what users dislike about XX.”
- “Find pain points in the XX space.”
- “Research market opportunities in XX.”
