CX Workflow — Requirements Gathering and Scale Assessment. Triggered when the user mentions "new feature", "requirement", "PRD", "I want to build", "help me plan", "gather requirements", or "feature planning". Uses multi-turn dialog to collect requirements, automatically assesses scale as S/M/L, saves to local file .claude/cx/features/{dev_id}-{feature}/prd.md, and intelligently routes based on scale: S→cx-plan, M/L→cx-design.
