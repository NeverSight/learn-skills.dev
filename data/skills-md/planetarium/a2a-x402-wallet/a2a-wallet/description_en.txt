Use the a2a-wallet CLI to interact with A2A agents — send messages, stream responses, and manage tasks. Also supports x402 payment signing and SIWE authentication required by A2A agents. Trigger when the user needs to: send a message to an A2A agent, sign an x402 payment, authenticate via SIWE, log in or out of a2a-wallet, check their wallet address or balance, or configure the a2a-wallet CLI.
