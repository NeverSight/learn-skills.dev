Reviews and critiques a CV/resume against proven best practices, providing a structured checklist with severity-tiered feedback and actionable suggestions for each item. Use this skill whenever a user asks to review, critique, check, improve, or get feedback on their CV or resume — even if they just say "look at my CV", "is my resume good?", "what's wrong with my resume?", or paste their CV content directly into the chat. Adapts feedback based on the user's experience level (student, mid-level, senior). Always use this skill when a CV or resume is present in the conversation.
