Universal video downloader. Based on yt-dlp, supports downloading and saving videos locally from 1000+ sites including YouTube, Bilibili, Twitter/X, Douyin, Xiaohongshu, etc. yt-dlp dependency will be installed automatically on first use. Triggered when the user provides a video link, requests a download, or mentions "save video" or "download video". Supports specifying output directory, choosing video quality, downloading audio only, etc.
