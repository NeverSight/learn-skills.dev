Create image-based PowerPoint decks by (1) designing a slide plan, (2) generating one 16:9 infographic slide image per slide with all text baked into the image (Chinese/English supported), and (3) assembling an images-only .pptx that simply concatenates those images full-screen. Use when the user wants polished, consistent visuals with extensible style packs (cinematic, editorial, warm pastoral, tech, youth social, academic, corporate), prefers not to hand-layout PPT objects, or wants a repeatable prompt workflow to iterate over time.
