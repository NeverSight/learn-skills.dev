A research skill that gathers and analyzes facts and opinions through quantitative and qualitative investigations on a given theme or issue, providing insights for decision-making. It advances the investigation by deepening the theme through brainstorming with users and delivers the findings as structured reports or recommendations.

Always activate in the following situations:
- When asked to “look into,” “research,” “investigate,” or “tell me about 〇〇”
- When market trends, technology trends, competitive analysis, industry trends, or social issues need to be examined
- When someone says “I want to brainstorm,” “let’s think together,” “I need to sort out the issues,” or “please organize the information”
- When comparing and organizing multiple perspectives or options for decision-making
- When wanting to grasp the current state, challenges, and outlook on a specific theme
