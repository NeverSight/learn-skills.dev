Discover valuable content semantically related to users’ interests from Hatena Bookmark. Persistently store users’ interests as profiles to improve future discovery accuracy. Triggered by requests like “Show me recommended articles on Hatena Bookmark,” “Look up today’s trends,” or “Find articles I’m interested in.” Subcommands: add <topic> / remove <topic> / list / view. Content discovery runs with no arguments or keyword input. Discovery results for the day are cached in Markdown and appended on multiple runs.
