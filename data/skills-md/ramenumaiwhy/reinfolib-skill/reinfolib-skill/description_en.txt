Automatically analyzes price per tsubo and price per unit for land and buildings using the Ministry of Land, Infrastructure, Transport and Tourism’s real-estate transaction data, starting from just an address. Useful for checking market rates and validating whether a property’s price is reasonable. Triggers on: 'price per tsubo', 'price per unit', 'check the market rate', 'is this land expensive?', 'real-estate price', 'check land prices', 'transaction examples', 'land market rate'. Do NOT use for: Google Maps searches (→ gemini-maps-grounding) or land-use zoning checks (→ use mlit-geospatial MCP directly).
