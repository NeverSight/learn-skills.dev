Knowledge Smith: Used to losslessly merge or append new hands-on experience (newly stepped pitfalls, newly discovered boundaries, or better solutions) into existing knowledge cards. The core principle is “extremely conservative micro-surgery”—strictly forbids deleting or over-trimming high-value experience (pain symptoms, wrong-answer logs) in the original asset, upgrading the old asset only by appending (Append) and structurally merging (Merge). Trigger signals: `/asset-evolve`, `/asset-update`
