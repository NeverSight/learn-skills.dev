A skill that automatically computes basic statistics for a DataFrame. Use this skill whenever a user uploads table-format data such as CSV, Excel, or Parquet, or mentions phrases like “basic statistics,” “descriptive statistics,” “describe,” “EDA,” “data summary,” “check distribution,” “check missing values,” or “profiling.” It covers data exploration, variable-type detection, missing-value inspection, tailored statistics for continuous, integer, categorical, and date types, and generation of a ydata-profiling report. Even for vague requests like “take a look at the data” or “analyze this file,” trigger this skill first.
