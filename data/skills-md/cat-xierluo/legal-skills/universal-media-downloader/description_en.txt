After entering links from various video sites or podcast platforms, the media files are automatically downloaded and delivered to the user. yt-dlp is preferred to cover common video sites like Douyin, Bilibili, and YouTube, and it also works for podcast platforms that expose direct audio URLs (e.g., Xiaoyu episode links). When encountering 403/login/age or region restrictions, cookies.txt can be used to retry; for platforms that may have DRM/encryption or licensing limits (e.g., some Spotify content), users should be reminded to download only content they are entitled to save, and if downloading is impossible, suggest using official offline/export options or provide the original RSS/direct link. Note: Douyin photo-text notes are not supported for automatic download and require manual handling.
