Convert legal texts (statutes or case law) into standard Markdown format, removing promotional and redundant content. Use this skill when users need to process statutes (e.g., Civil Code, Criminal Law), organize legal cases (e.g., Supreme Court model cases, court decisions), or format legal documents from pasted text. Note: This skill only formats and cleans content; it does not scrape content. Content acquisition should be handled by other skills (e.g., wechat-article-fetch), and the AI will automatically determine the sequence of skill collaboration.
