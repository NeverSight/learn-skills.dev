Triages findings from Telegram, articles, posts, YouTube videos — explains the gist in plain language, maps to user's current projects, and recommends an action. Use when the user shares a post, link, screenshot, or file and wants to understand if it's useful. Accepts any format: post text, website/GitHub/YouTube link, screenshot, meeting transcript (.md, .txt). Triggers: "look what I found", "triage this", "check this out", "what should I do with this", "is this useful", or when the user pastes a link/text that looks like an external finding. Second mode: "review my ideas", "what's in the ideas folder" — review saved ideas.
