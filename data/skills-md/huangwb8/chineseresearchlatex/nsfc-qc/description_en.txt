Used when the user explicitly requests "bid QC/quality control/pre-polishing quality inspection/citation authenticity check/length and structure review." Performs read-only quality control on NSFC proposals: multi-threaded parallel independent checks for stiff writing, risk of fake or incorrect citations, length and chapter distribution, logical clarity, etc., and finally outputs a standardized QC report; intermediate files are archived by default to the "hidden workspace within the delivery directory (.nsfc-qc/)" and are compatible with legacy `.nsfc-qc/`.
