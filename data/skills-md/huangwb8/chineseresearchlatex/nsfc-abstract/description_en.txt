When the user explicitly requests “write/polish an NSFC proposal abstract,” “generate both Chinese and English abstracts,” or “translate the Chinese abstract into English.”

Output both Chinese and English versions: the English must be a faithful translation of the Chinese (no added information, no omission of key constraints).
Chinese abstract defaults to ≤400 characters (including punctuation), English abstract defaults to ≤4000 characters (including punctuation); final limits follow `skills/nsfc-abstract/config.yaml:limits`.

Output method: write the results into `NSFC-ABSTRACTS.md` in the **working directory** (filename follows `config.yaml:output.filename`), with one `#` heading for Chinese and one `#` heading for English.

⚠️ Not applicable in the following cases:
- The user only wants to translate a generic text unrelated to the proposal (translate directly)
- The user only wants to write the rationale/research content/research foundation text (use corresponding nsfc series skill)
