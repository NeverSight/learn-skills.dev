Distill AI chat content into structured technical documents and save them to your Obsidian knowledge base. Supports three document types: problem-solving, knowledge-learning, and solution-comparison. Automatically infers the vault folder path and generates Markdown files compliant with Obsidian standards (including frontmatter and backlinks). Ideal for: (1) recording a resolved technical issue from a chat, (2) archiving newly learned knowledge into your vault, (3) preserving the decision-making process after comparing several solutions. Triggered when the user says: “save to knowledge base,” “export to Obsidian,” “record to notes,” “save this conversation,” “convert to document,” etc.
